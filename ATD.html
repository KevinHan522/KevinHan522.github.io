Project Description

Arcane Tower Defense is a tower defense game with a deep layer of strategy and a large variety of towers and enemies. Like all other tower defense games, you have a limited amount of money to place towers and a certain life count that is reduced every time an enemy makes it to the end. Each tower has its own specialization, ranging from high damaging fire towers to multi-hitting earth towers. Enemies also range from high speed yellow slimes to high defense gray ones. You also have options to upgrade towers to improve shot frequency, tower range, and even unlock new shot types. Selling and moving tower functionality is also implemented.

This project was done throughout my freshman year at Georgia Tech with C# and XNA. It was a big challenge to implement the features I wanted. While there are still a few unimplemented shots, I am proud of the functionality I have done. The game was my first real personal project and was definitely one of the most fun to work for.

Features

  • Varied tower types
  • Varied enemy types
  • Upgrading, selling, and moving towers
